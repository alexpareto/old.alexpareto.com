Wondering how it works?


We partner with students who have lots of extra dining dollars. We Tapingo orders for students (like you) with their dining dollars. You venmo us (@GrubBucks) only 75% of the cost of the original order. We pass this cash on to the students with dining dollars.

Students get cash for their extra dining dollars at a small loss. You get cheaper food on campus.

Everyone wins.
